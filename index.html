Progressive overload is the golden rule. Focus on gradually increasing the weight and intensity of your workouts.

Base your workouts on compound lifts like:

  • Squats
  • Deadlifts
  • Bench Press
  • Pull-ups

üìä Training Styles

There are several approaches to weight training depending on your goals:

  • Hypertrophy (8-12 reps): Maximizes muscle size
  • Strength (4-6 reps): Focuses on maximum force output
  • Endurance (15+ reps): Builds stamina and definition

Smart lifters rotate styles in a technique known as periodization.
